Old Fashioned

  • bourbon
  • sugar cube
  • soda
  • Fee Brothers bitters

GLASS: BUCKET

  1. place ORANGE SLICE, 1 CHERRY AND SUGAR CUBE at bottom of GLASS
  2. saturate SUGAR CUBE with two dashes of FEE BROTHERS BITTERS
  3. gently muddle.
  4. add two dashes of WATER
  5. FILL WITH ICE
  6. 2 oz BOURBON
  7. splash SODA

GARNISH: CHERRY, SPOON OF CHERRY SYRUP
